2019 World Economic Freedom

This visualization shows some maps and diagrams about world ecomoic situation and some data that closely related to countries' economic performance

World Index of Economic Freedom

This is Choropleth map of the World Index of Economic Freedom. The Economic Freedom is the fundamental right of every human to control his or her ownlabor and property. The Index of Economic Freedom, an annual report released by the Wall Street Journal and the American Heritage Foundation, covers 186 countries and regions around the world and is one of the world's authoritative indicators of economic freedom. The higher the score on an index, the higher the level of government intervention in the economy, and therefore the lower the level of economic freedom.Here is the five economic freedom levels.

World Fiscal Health

Fiancial health is the ability of a country to manage its finances and it is shown by the Fiscal Health Score on the map. It also has the relation to a country's Financial Freedom Score, Labor Freedom Score and Tax Burden etc. These figures clearly reflect the financial situation of a country's government and people. Then you can find these data from the tooltip.

Economic Freedom Score VS Business Freedom

This bubble plot shows the relationship between the Economic Freedom Score and business freedom situation. You can find out as the Economic Freedom score increase the corresbonding country has higher Business Freedom Score. It means this country has better commercial environment. And the size of the bubble shows the GDP of ths country.

Economic Freedom Score VS GDP per Capita

This bubble plot shows the relationship of Economic Freedom Score and GDP per Capita. The countries with higher Economic Freedom usually have higher GDP(per capita). And we can find out this does not have relationship with the country size by looking at the size of the bubble.

GDP Growth Rate

The GDP growth rate is the percentage change in the value of all of the goods and services produced in a nation during a specific period of time, as compared to an earlier period. This also reflect the ecomoic freedom change of a country. This diverging bar chart shows the top 10 positive change and worst 10 negeative change of the world country.
